How to cook white rice
======================
Step 1: Go buy rice for village square, gwallameji
Step 2: Wash the rice
Step 3: Turn on your stove
Step 4: Pour water in a pot
Step 5: put the pot on the stove
Step 6: Pour the washed rice in the pot
step 7: Add some pinches of salt
Step 8: Allow to cook for 15 mins
Step 9: check after 10mins to see if rice is soft
Step 10: When rice is soft, quench the fire
Step 11: Serve with stew, or fried groudnut oil
Step 12: Serve along with chilled kunun geda
